Cornbread recipe

Description

Cornbread is a traditional southern food. It's been around for a long time. There is a reason why people eat it to This day and it is because it is good. This is a recipe to make delicious cornbread that will leave you wanting more. Perfect for a side dish (especially with chili) or a quick snack!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Yelow cornmeal
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Two eggs
  • Pure maple syrup or honey
  • Whole milk greek yogurt
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Get a 9x9 inch pan or a 8x8 inch pan and either butter it of spray with nonstick cooking spray
  2. Add all of the dry ingredients to a bowl and whisk them together
  3. In a separate bowl mix together all of your wet ingredients besides the melted butter until well-combined.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and stir together until your batter forms. Stir in the butter last and be sure to not overmix!
  5. Bake your cornbread for 18-25 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Serve warm with butter & honey!
